Information & Rules

__________________________

-Use of the DOLA requires a valid DOLA pass as well as a valid park pass for all vehicles entering the park.

-Hours 5am to 10pm

-You MUST have your dog within visual distance and under voice control when off leash.

-A leash and a waste bag MUST be carried for each dog.

-Cleaning up after your pet is mandatory, as it is crucial to the preservation and cleanliness of the DOLA and watershed.
